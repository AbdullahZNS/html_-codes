Md.Abu Shadik Kayem


Md. Abu Shadik Kayem is a Bangladeshi student activist and politician who currently serves as the Vice President (VP) of the Dhaka University Central Students’ Union (DUCSU). He is widely recognized as a leader connected with the 2024 “July Revolution” student movement, where he played a coordinating role and later became one of its prominent public faces. He is also identified as a leading figure of Bangladesh Islami Chhatra Shibir, where he has held organizational leadership positions, including being associated with its DU unit leadership. He has been described in public discussions as the president/leader of the BICS (Shibir-affiliated student platform) during the post-movement period. In 2025, he was elected DUCSU VP in a major campus election, gaining significant attention in national student politics. He is considered one of the most influential young student leaders in Bangladesh’s recent university political landscape.
